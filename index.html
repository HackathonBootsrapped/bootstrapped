We’re just a couple of average Joe’s who created a platform that allows other average Joe’s to promote their skills and connect to their local community. We give you an internet presence without the hassle of navigating complicated tech so that your clients can find you easily & request your services. We help promote your specific skill set to a larger market so that you can get paid more often for doing Odd Jobs for your neighbours!

With Odd Jobs, you’ll be able to build a profile, list your skills, and post photos of your work so that people in your area can find and book you easily. We manage your payments, offering instalment options for the buyer and reassurance for the worker.

How it works for an Average Joe

  • Build a profile
  • List your skills
  • Upload pictures of your work
  • Set your schedule
  • Sit back and let your odd jobs find you!

How it works for the Client

  • Search for the skill you need i.e carpentry, child care, etc.
  • Recieve a list of Average Joe's in your area offering those skills.
  • Review their profile, scroll throught their photo gallery and read testimonials from past clients
  • Request a quote
  • Book online
  • Pay securily through our website
